Andrea Castaneda

MEET OUR MEMBERS Andrea Castaneda was born in Honduras in 1991 in the city of Tegucigalpa. She is a a muralist, fine artist and illustrator that started to be known as a muralist in the Roatan island. Her work focuses on the environment, endangered species, global awareness, and human rights. It spreads a pro-democracy message and advocates for peace and disarmament. Her native country of Honduras inspires her to paint the beauty of her natural surroundings and celebrate Honduran and world ecology and culture. She usually paints elder people that for her represent the Earth. Her works has been exhibited internationally and made murals in Mexico, Washington DC.
