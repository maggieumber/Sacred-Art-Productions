Maggie Umber

MEET OUR FRIENDS Maggie Umber paints, prints, and programs comics, and she's a co-founder of the alternative comics publishing label 2dcloud. She's published three graphic novels — Sound of Snow Falling, Time Capsule, and 270° — and her work has been widely anthologized. The Man in the Blue Suit, her fourth graphic novel, is forthcoming from 2dcloud.
