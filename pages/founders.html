Vita Rhie Quintanilla

is an artist, musician, author, and student of genetics from San Francisco, California. Her work reflects a seeking, shared by artists and scientists alike for revelation along the path of the unknown. Her interdisciplinary repertoire represents the experience of growing up in the margins of the American melting-pot designated to the black sheep of an evolving nation and globalizing world.
