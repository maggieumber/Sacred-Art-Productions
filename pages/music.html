Salvation Day

MEET OUR MEMBERS

Vita Rhie Quintanilla and Anthony Tadlock are Salvation Day, a San Francisco Alternative Rock and Blues Duo. Formed in 2019 the group draws on material from classic jazz standards to original compositions. Salvation Day started as Vita Quintanilla and The Notorious T, playing coffee shops and open mics in San Francisco. The band then rebranded as Salvation Day after recording their first studio album Path of Sacred Art and forming the record label and artist's collaborative network Sacred Art Productions. They released their first EP, a three-song concept album, The Backdoor Sessions in 2020.

Path of Sacred Art

Salvation Day's debut full length album is Path of Sacred Art. It was released at the end of 2020. It contains eight songs: The Tax We Pay, San Francisco, Para Ti, Reincarnation, Mercy Sunrise, Clear Water, Sacred Art (Hidden Track).
