The Schizophrenic Dialogues

MAR. 23, 2020

The Schizophrenic Dialogues (Vita R. Quintanilla) are a collection of Memoirs on the first hand experience of schizophrenia told through the lens of the actual dialogue between a person with schizophrenia and her auditory Visual and tactile hallucinations. These accounts and their company and commentary represent the reality of people with schizophrenia in society and within themselves. The dialogues chatelet on a life harnessing a stigmatized condition as a positive in defiance of a grave prognosis. Get yours today!
