Salvation Day at Ocean Beach Cafe

FEB. 19, 2022

On February 19, Salvation Day played an hour set at Ocean Beach Cafe in San Francisco, CA. Thank you to all of our friends and fans that were able to attend.

Want to hear more? Salvation Day's debut full length album is Path of Sacred Art. It was released at the end of 2020. It contains eight songs: The Tax We Pay, San Francisco, Para Ti, Reincarnation, Mercy Sunrise, Clear Water, Sacred Art (Hidden Track).
