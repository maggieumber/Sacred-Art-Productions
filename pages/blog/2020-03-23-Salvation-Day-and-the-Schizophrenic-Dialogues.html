Salvation Day & The Schizophrenic Dialogues

MAR. 23, 2020

COVID 19 may be preventing shows like this one from happening now but the music certainly will not. Salvation Day's first release will be coming out March 2020!



The Schizophrenic Dialogues by Vita R. Quintanilla are a collection of Memoirs on the first hand experience of schizophrenia told through the lens of the actual dialogue between a person with schizophrenia and her auditory Visual and tactile hallucinations. These accounts and their company and commentary represent the reality of people with schizophrenia in society and within themselves. The dialogues chatelet on a life harnessing a stigmatized condition as a positive in defiance of a grave prognosis. Get yours today!
