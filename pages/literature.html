Joyce Yu

MEET OUR MEMBERS Joyce Wu is a poet based in California, USA. Her passion for writing stemmed from her love of reading, and it has grown ever since she realized that to her, Art was therapy. She suffers from a number of chronic illnesses and finds that writing about her struggles not only helps her out of dark times but also provides support for the many people struggling with their own demons.

Noah Humphrey

MEET OUR MEMBERS Noah Humphrey (Knowa Know) recently graduated from Whittier College a B.A in Religious Studies and a minor in Holistic Care. He is now based in Oahu, Hawaii. He uses poetry as a second voice and infuses personal experiences in South Central Los Angeles, African-American heritage, nature, and holistic elements into them. He lets his soul speak through poetry and believes that with peace, passion, patience, support, and God anything is possible through this path called life.

Vita Rhie Quintanilla

The Schizophrenic Dialogues is a collection of memoirs on the first-hand experience of schizophrenia told through the lens of the actual dialogue between a person with schizophrenia and her auditory, visual, and tactile hallucinations. These accounts and their accompanying commentary represent the reality of people with schizophrenia in society and within themselves. The Dialogues shed light on the lives built to harness a stigmatized condition as a positive, and live in defiance of a "grave" prognosis.

MEET OUR MEMBERS Vita Rhie Quintanilla is the author of the Schizophrenic Dialogues, Writer and Frontwoman of Salvation Day and cofounder of Sacred Art Produtions.

Crystal Garcia

MEET OUR MEMBERS Crystal Garcia is a writer and content creator from Corpus Christi, Texas. She especially enjoys indulging in poetry, however also writes engaging content, articles, and essays. Crystal is a lover of words and literature—once she begins reading or writing, it can be difficult for her to stop. At a young age, she has found immense solace in writing to express herself. She is a writer who is explicitly transparent and seeks to engender excitement and awe in readers while creating a calm within herself.
